Hi, I hope you're doing well.

I was born and raised in the beautiful foothills of the Himalayan Mountains, where I developed a deep appreciation for nature and the outdoors. The rugged peaks and verdant valleys of the Himalayas are a constant source of inspiration for me, and I have always felt a deep connection to the land.

Growing up in a rural community, I was exposed to the simple pleasures of farm life. From a young age, I helped my family tend to our small plot of land, where we grew a variety of crops and raised livestock. The hard working and close-knit community of our village taught me the value of the importance of supporting one another.

In my spare time, you can find me playing badminton or going on hikes with friends. The rustic and rural background of my upbringing has shaped who I am and continues to inspire my work and passions.
